Giacomo Meanti

I am a PostDoc in the Thoth team, at Inria Grenoble with Julien Mairal. I completed my PhD at the University of Genova, supervised by Lorenzo Rosasco.

My research interests include

  • Inverse problems for imaging. I'm interested in novel approaches to solving inverse problems. Recently I've been studying how to deal with the case when the forward model is unknown, and only unpaired datasets are available.
  • Developing efficient algorithms for shallow learning. I developed the Falkon library for scaling up kernel ridge regression, as well as the K-Planes algorithm for representing neural radiance fields with shallow neural networks.
  • Developing kernel methods and shallow learning algorithms for scientific applications. For example dynamical systems, wind forecasting, molecular dynamics.
